About Me


CODER

Though I’ve enjoyed playing around with HTML since the early days of Angelfire and MySpace, it wasn’t until a friend asked me to create her photography website a few years ago that I began to think about web development as a viable career option. Now, I can’t stop...

CREATOR

I have many hobbies, but the one thing they all have in common is the creative process. Whether I’m coding a new website, crocheting a baby blanket for a friend (hello, 30s!), learning to cover one of my favorite artists (currently Dermot Kennedy ) on guitar, or creating a cartoon version of my cat for Instagram (@LittleWhiteWhisker), it’s the creative side of my brain that I fall back on.

TEACHER

Once described as a closet nerd, I have always loved reading, writing, and learning. Raised bilingual, and passionate about language since I was a kid, the language classroom was always the most exciting place for me to be as a student. It was inevitable, then, that I would pursue a career in language education. While I’m ready to make a shift to the tech industry at this point in my life, my 15+ years as a language teacher gave me a wealth of experience in international contexts - and made me an adept communicator with a knack for starting conversations with absolutely anyone.

LINGUIST

I went to my first French class kicking and screaming at my mother’s insistence, but three years later when I went to Paris for the first time and surprised myself by being able to communicate with everyone in an entirely different language, I was HOOKED. Since then, language learning has been a steady passion of mine. I am Spanish/English bilingual, and have studied French, Italian, Portuguese, Arabic, and, most recently, Czech.
